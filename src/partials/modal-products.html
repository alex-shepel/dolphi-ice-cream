ice cream

Creamy ice cream is a delicacy that is very popular in almost everything the world. This chilled dessert is made with milk and cream.

Useful properties

The benefits of ice cream are due to the rich composition of vitamins and minerals. There is in this dessert phosphorus, which is actively involved in protein synthesis and in the formation of bone and muscle tissue.

This product contains a large amount of calcium, which strengthens and improves the condition of hair, nails, teeth and bones. Thanks to potassium, which is also in creamy ice cream, the activity of the cardiovascular system is normalized.

ice coffee

Iced coffee is the perfect solution to the problem. You are drinking your favorite drink and it is a great thirst quencher. Coffee with ice cream began to be prepared in the 19th century in Austria, then the recipe with changes migrated to Italy and France, after which it spread throughout the world.

Choice of flavors

Glase is a popular espresso-based coffee drink with the addition of ice cream. The name of this cold dessert is French, but it is believed that the drink was invented in Austria.

In Italy, a popular dessert called affogato is a ball of ice cream poured with strong hot coffee. The composition of the dessert often includes liqueur or cognac. You can use a high-quality ice cream, but there are lovers who choose chocolate or nut ice cream.
